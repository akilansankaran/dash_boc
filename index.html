Two nations, one question: immigration within and across the U.S. and Canada.

Developed at Harvard by Charles River Economics Labs, this tracker monitors immigration flows, policy frameworks, labor market integration, and fiscal outcomes across the United States and Canada.

Methodology: We have integrated standardized, cross-nationally comparable metrics derived from administrative records, census microdata, cross-sectional datasets, and longitudinal surveys to provide consistent measures of immigration patterns and outcomes across both countries.
